Congratulations to Chaos Lacrosse Club for winning the 2021 Playoffs Championship Game!
Won 14-9 against the Whipsnakes in the Championships

About Chaos Lacrosse Club: 2021 Champs

Chaos Lacrosse Club was coached by Andy Towers, Matt Panetta, and Ryan Curtis. Their top scorers this year were:
Attackman Josh Byrne - 29pts,
Midfielder Dhane Smith - 20pts,
and Attackman Chris Cloutier - 19pts.

Chaos' Defense was also very strong, with
Defenseman Jack Rowlett - 14 turnovers,
Defenseman Johnny Surdick - 13 turnovers,
and Jarrod Neumann - 11 turnovers.

Also, their goalie, Blaze Riorden with 61% of shots saved dominated the 2021 season.

Last, but not least, their faceoff man Max Adler won about 49% of his faceoffs.
